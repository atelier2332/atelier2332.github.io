Milk

Initiated in march 2017, Milk is an augmented land art experience set on two pentagonal pyramids called "Les Pyramides" (Lille - FR).

What ?

Milk is an Android application.
It's augmented reality cuz I placed geolocalized virtual audiosources on the pyramids.
It's an experience cuz the music lives & breathe as you wander about the pyramids.

What do I do ?

1. Download the app (Android only) & install it (you need a gyro + GPS on your freaking phone !).

2. Go to "Les Pyramides". You have to go to this specific place to activate the app. Anywhere else on earth the app will NOT work & you'll get a black screen. When the app is activated, your screen will display a sweet & soothing pink.

3. Now you hear the music.
Plug your headphones, it's a binaural experience.
Each virtual audiosource contains one track, so the main "mix" depends on your precise location.
Walk, listen, and do whatever you want.
